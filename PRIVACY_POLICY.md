# HealthLogiq Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

HealthLogiq is designed to help users view and understand selected Apple Health metrics in a simple, private, and informative way.

## Data Access

HealthLogiq requests permission to read selected Apple Health data, such as activity, sleep, heart, recovery, and fitness metrics. The app uses this data to display tiles, charts, trends, baselines, and health insights.

HealthLogiq does not request Health data access until the user grants permission through Apple Health authorization.

## Data Use

Health data is used inside the app to:

- Show health metric tiles
- Build trend charts
- Compare current values with recent personal medians
- Provide wellness summaries and chat-based interpretation

HealthLogiq is for wellness insights only. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent medical conditions.

## Data Sharing

HealthLogiq does not sell health data.

HealthLogiq does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or external health-data upload features.

On supported devices, HealthLogiq may use Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework to help interpret metrics in chat. If Apple Intelligence is not available, the app uses local rule-based insights.

## Data Storage

HealthLogiq does not create a separate permanent health-data database. Health values are read from Apple Health and displayed in the app. Chat messages are kept only in app memory during use and are limited to recent messages.

## User Control

Users can manage or revoke Health access at any time in the iOS Settings app under Health permissions.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
